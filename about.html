Who We Are?

MNH represents a group of talented and creative individuals, bringing together Nabil with his three years of experience in design and video editing, Hamza with his one-year expertise in website programming, Zouhir with six months of experience in social media management, Zakaria with his one-year background in 3D model design, and Mohamed, possessing two years of expertise in developing embroidery techniques on clothing. These individuals work as a cohesive team complementing each other to produce intricately embroidered garments of high quality. The fusion of creativity and attention to detail results in a unique experience in the world of fashion and design.
